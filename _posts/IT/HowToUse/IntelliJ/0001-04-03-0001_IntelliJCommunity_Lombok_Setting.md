> IntelliJ(인텔리J) Community 버전에서 Lombok 설정하기

> ### Lombok 설정     

___

&nbsp; 
<b> 1) **`File - Settings - Plugins`** : Lombok을 검색하여 install후 IntelliJ를 재시작한다. </b>     
<br />
![lombok install](/resources/images/HowToUse/IntelliJ/lombok001.PNG "lombok install")
<br /><br />


&nbsp; 
<b> 2) **`File - Settings - Build, Execution, Deployment - Complier - Annotaion Processions`** 로 이동하여      
&nbsp;&nbsp; 
[Enable annotation processing] 항목을 체크한다. </b>
<br />           
![lombok install](/resources/images/HowToUse/IntelliJ/lombok002.PNG "lombok install")
<br /><br />


&nbsp; 
<b> 3) Lombok 설정 완료 </b>
